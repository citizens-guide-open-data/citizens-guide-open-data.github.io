What is the Citizen's Guide?

We are preparing a Citizen’s Guide to Open Data (citizens-guide-open-data.github.io) that provides citizens with tools to understand what makes up open data, how it can be used in their communities, and where to find it. It is designed to be used by citizens, community groups, and students seeking to learn open data stories, concepts, and tools.

The Citizen’s Guide effort is set to launch Fall 2016 as part of Geothink and is lead by Professor Leslie Shade at University of Toronto and assisted by Masters students Curtis McCord and Dawn Walker.

If you are interested, you can read our current draft guide (goo.gl/jiEkaM) and open data personas (https://goo.gl/2Q4Lku)!
